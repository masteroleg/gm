# Message and Claim Rules for Notebook LM

## Approved narrative order

- implementation is inevitable;
- the buyer's real question is safe rollout;
- `genu.mark` is the strong core;
- `ERP` is external in two roles;
- `еАкциз` is a separate external contour;
- strong core first reduces launch risk;
- `Dominanta + Logictime` is the safer path;
- next step = audit / architecture session / pilot.

## Approved lines

- `genu.mark is the strong line core for еАкциз implementation.`
- `ERP -> source of production job.`
- `ERP / internal systems <- exchange of results and statuses.`
- `еАкциз is a separate external contour.`
- `Strong core first reduces launch risk.`
- `Public proof does not replace official or legal verification.`

## Banned lines

- any opening that sells platform instead of `genu.mark`;
- any wording that merges regulated and voluntary logic;
- any early mention of `genu.code`;
- `ERP` or `еАкциз` as part of the core;
- `genu.im` as the first thing being sold;
- guarantees, rank claims, or compliance promises.

## Tone rules

Tone must be:
- calm;
- modern;
- buyer-facing;
- operational;
- email-readable.

Tone must not be:
- theatrical;
- taxonomy-first;
- operator-like;
- draft-like.
