# Role and Ownership Map

## Канонічна формула для regulated motion

`Domino equipment + genu.mark = ready customer path for еАкциз`

## Хто є хто

### Dominanta

- Domino equipment;
- trusted commercial front;
- first conversation with the customer.

### Domino

- hardware layer;
- marking equipment;
- entry point into the production contour.

### Logictime

- architecture;
- integration;
- exceptions;
- implementation;
- launch.

### genu.mark

- main object of sale in regulated motion;
- line core;
- receives codes as input;
- code application;
- verification;
- aggregation;
- packaging result.

### ERP

External contour in two roles:
- source of production job;
- exchange of results and statuses.

### еАкциз

Separate external state contour.

### genu.im

Supporting proof context only when needed.

### genu.code

Separate voluntary branch.

Not part of the opening regulated motion.

## Як правильно пояснювати клієнту

- Доминанта приводить клієнта через Domino equipment;
- Logictime збирає і запускає контур навколо `genu.mark`;
- зовнішні блоки підключаються окремо;
- proof context не підміняє product core.

## Як не можна пояснювати

- `genu.im is the solution`;
- `Domino solves the whole еАкциз problem`;
- `ERP is part of the core`;
- `еАкциз is built into the product core`;
- `genu.code` as part of the early regulated offer.
