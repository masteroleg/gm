## Required structure

Use exactly this 8-slide order by default:

1. inevitable `–Ķ–ź–ļ—Ü–ł–∑` launch and the buyer question about safe implementation
2. `genu.mark` as the line core
3. `ERP` and `–Ķ–ź–ļ—Ü–ł–∑` as external contours
4. strong core lowers rollout risk
5. interface and exception risk lives on the handoffs
6. `Dominanta + Logictime` as safer path
7. operational outcome
8. next step CTA

Slide 3 must contain both exact ERP lines:
- `ERP -> source of production job`
- `ERP / internal systems <- exchange of results and statuses`

Slide 5 must make handoff and exception risk concrete, not generic:
- interface failures;
- offline / recovery;
- reject / rework;
- aggregation breaks or packaging exceptions.

## Editorial standard

- calm and industrial, not theatrical;
- concise slide titles;
- no draft notes, bridge lines, or operator wording;
- no inflated superiority language;
- no `—Ē–ī–ł–Ĺ–į –≤—Ė–ī–Ņ–ĺ–≤—Ė–ī–į–Ľ—Ć–Ĺ—Ė—Ā—ā—Ć –∑–į —Ä–Ķ–∑—É–Ľ—Ć—ā–į—ā`, `–Ĺ–į–Ļ–ļ—Ä–į—Č–Ķ –ĺ–Ī–Ľ–į–ī–Ĺ–į–Ĺ–Ĺ—Ź`, or equivalent overclaims;
- the buyer should understand the deck without a presenter filling gaps.
