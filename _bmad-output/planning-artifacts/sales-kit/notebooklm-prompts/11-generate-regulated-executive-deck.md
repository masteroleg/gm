Use only the uploaded sources. Do not invent architecture, benefits, claims, or steps that are not supported by those sources.

## Hard rules

- Sell `genu.mark` first.
- Treat `genu.mark` as the line core.
- Make the first screen and first paragraph clearly sell `genu.mark` as the safe path to `еАкциз` implementation.
- Render `genu.mark` as the simple strong core that receives codes, applies, verifies, aggregates, and gives packaging result.
- Treat `ERP` as external in two explicit roles:
  - `ERP -> source of production job`
  - `ERP / internal systems <- exchange of results and statuses`
- Treat `еАкциз` as a separate external contour.
- Do not open with `genu.im`.
- Do not mention `genu.code` in the early regulated narrative.
- Do not use platform-first, ecosystem-first, or shared-core wording.
- Do not introduce any new product, architecture layer, product branch, platform explanation, or selling object that is not explicitly present in the uploaded sources.
- Keep the output in modern, buyer-facing Ukrainian.
- End with a clear next step to audit / architecture session / pilot.
- Do not use vague wording like only `ERP integration`.
- Do not use unsafe claims or superiority language such as `найкраще`, `№1`, `під ключ`, `гарантує`, `повністю закриває`, `єдина відповідальність за результат`, `гарантований комплаєнс`, `single responsibility`, `full package`, `best equipment`, `turnkey`.
- Do not show NotebookLM branding, watermark, service logo, product chrome, generator badge, UI residue, or document-reference residue anywhere in the visible output.

## Output contract

Return 10-12 slides by default. You may compress to 8-9 slides only if no mandatory meaning is lost. You may expand to 13-14 slides only if each additional slide closes a distinct mandatory buying-question. Use this format for each slide: `Slide N - title`, `Key message`, `3-5 bullets`, `visual note`, `speaker line`.

Keep slide numbering clean. Do not insert broken references, footnote numbers, placeholders, or artifacts like `1`, `more_horiz`, or source markers inside the text.

- it sells a platform instead of `genu.mark`;
- it hides the ERP split under vague integration language;
- it shows `ERP` or `еАкциз` as core;
- it introduces any unsourced product, platform layer, or architecture concept;
- it skips interface and exception risk;
- it expands past 12 slides without a clear distinct buying-question per extra slide;
- it uses inflated or unsafe claims;
- it paraphrases the required ERP lines instead of printing them exactly;
- it contains broken numbering or notebook artifacts;
- it contains NotebookLM branding, watermark, service chrome, logo, badge, or document-reference residue;

- it lacks a clear CTA;
