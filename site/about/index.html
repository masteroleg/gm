About

What genu.im is — and what it is not.

A brand-controlled proof surface for marked products. Not a government service. Not a verification authority.

What we do

genu.im gives manufacturers and brands a way to attach a checkable record to each marked product unit. When a buyer, partner, or auditor scans the code, they see proof published by the brand — not a guess, not a generic page.

What we are not

genu.im is not a government service and does not perform state verification. Official eAktsyz checks happen through Diia. genu.im is a brand-layer platform that sits alongside that process.

How product trust works here

Each product code carries a linked record. The record is set by the brand. The scan opens that record for anyone with a phone. No app required. No account needed to verify.
