# Asset Intelligence Powered by Dynamic QR Infrastructure

URL Source: http://delivr.com/page/831/asset-intelligence-powered-by-dynamic-qr-infrastructure

Markdown Content:
For years, basic barcodes on equipment and parts have been limited to inventory management and shipment tracking. By replacing — or supplementing — the barcode with a “smart” QR code, manufacturers can store more data, streamline workflows, and make information accessible to operations, suppliers, warehouses, service teams, and customers across every touchpoint in the product lifecycle.

Combining QR codes with a modern digital encoding scheme and an experience portal unlocks nearly limitless possibilities. From Material Master and Batch/Lot to item-level serialization, this new digital link framework connects components, finished units, and shipments to rich information and services across the supply chain, product cloud, and other systems. The result — use cases that empower people to see, know, and do more at every stage of the product journey.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Designed for Operations, Suppliers, Warehouse, Service Teams, and Customers
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   **Operations.** See the entire product lifecycle in real time, track performance, and resolve bottlenecks to keep workflows running smoothly.
*   **Suppliers.** Monitor parts and shipments from production to installation, improving supply chain efficiency and customer satisfaction.
*   **Warehouse.** Track inventory accurately, reduce misplaced parts, and simplify daily operations for a more efficient workflow.
*   **Service.** Scan equipment on-site for instant access to manuals, troubleshooting, and resources, boosting field efficiency and problem-solving.
*   **Customers.** Link equipment to manuals, videos, warranties, and service requests, reducing downtime and making maintenance effortless.

Real-World Use Cases
--------------------

*   **Instant Asset Tracking.** Scan a part or piece of equipment to view its details, history, and location immediately.
*   **Maintenance Management.** Ensure timely servicing by linking QR codes to maintenance logs.
*   **Field Support.** Technicians access manuals, service guides, and on-site information to replace parts.
*   **Inventory Audits.** Count and verify stock quickly, eliminating manual errors.
*   **Returns and Spare Parts Tracking.** Streamline reverse logistics for faster replacements, repairs, and forensic analysis.

Create labels with QR codes for each part or piece of equipment.

➁

---------------------

Link QR codes to manuals, specifications, warranties, or service history.

➂

Scan Anywhere.
--------------

Smartphones or handheld scanners update inventory and status in real time.

➃

Monitor and Audit.
------------------

Track movement, usage, and availability in real time.

➄

Analyze and Optimize.
---------------------

Use scan data to enhance inventory management, reduce downtime, and prevent bottlenecks.

➅

Dynamic QR Codes.
-----------------

Update linked information at any time without reprinting labels. This feature not only saves time but also cuts costs associated with delays and reprinting. Imagine parts that move between warehouses and service vehicles; any change in their details is instant. By preventing downtime due to outdated information, dynamic QR codes help you avoid high operational costs and ensure the seamless movement of parts across multiple facilities.

---------------------------------------

*   **Reduce Errors.** Prevent incorrect parts from being installed or shipped.
*   **Save Time.** Scan items instead of logging them manually.
*   **Boost Visibility.** Instantly see the location of every part and piece of equipment.
*   **Improve Maintenance Compliance.** Ensure timely servicing of machines and equipment.
*   **Lower Costs.** Reduce downtime, overstocking, and lost items.

Fact. According to a study by the Logistics and Supply Chain Management Society, companies using QR codes for parts management reduce misplacements by up to 40% and improve maintenance compliance by 30%.
