Are GS1 Digital Link and Smart, Connected Packaging the same thing?
===================================================================

Are GS1 Digital Link and Smart, Connected Packaging the same thing?

**Not exactly—but they’re deeply connected.** Both help brands provide detailed product information via QR codes. While **GS1 Digital Link is a universal barcode standard**, **smart, connected packaging** is a broader concept that uses QR codes (and other triggers) to engage consumers, deliver transparency, and support traceability throughout the product's lifecycle.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

What is Smart, Connected Packaging?

Smart, connected packaging includes labels, inserts, and packaging that use QR codes to provide consumers with more than just printed information. This can include:

*   Ingredients, nutrition, and allergen details
*   Sustainability and sourcing information
*   Recipes, videos, and how-to guides
*   Product authentication or regulatory disclosures
*   Traceability and supply chain visibility

Platforms like SmartLabel® are often used as destinations, but the term extends beyond that — enabling brands to build trust, meet compliance requirements, and offer post-purchase engagement.

What is GS1 Digital Link?

**GS1 Digital Link** is a global standard that converts product identifiers (such as GTINs) into URLs. A single GS1 Digital Link-enabled QR code can:

*   Be scanned at retail POS
*   Connect to SmartLabel®, product pages, or instructions
*   Route based on language, location, or device
*   Enable product recalls, verification, or serialization
*   Support traceability, blockchain, and compliance

With **resolver logic**, you can future-proof packaging by updating destinations over time—without changing the code.

**Key Differences**
-------------------

**Feature****Smart, Connected Packaging****GS1 Digital Link**
**What it is**A way to connect physical packaging to digital content A global standard for web-enabled barcodes
**Purpose**Consumer Engagement, Product InformationTransparency, Traceability POS Transactions, Product Information, Supply Chain Traceability, Consumer Engagement
**QR type**Any QR (static or dynamic)Standards-based dynamic or static QR
**POS compatibility**Not always Yes
**Uses resolver?**Optional Optional
**Delivr support**Full ecosystem support Fully GS1-aligned

Why this matters.

More brands are adopting **GS1 Digital Link** as the backbone of their connected packaging strategy. It simplifies things by using **a single QR code** for both consumer and supply chain use—eliminating the need for multiple codes on pack.
