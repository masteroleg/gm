# Sell at your storefront, whether you're open or closed.

URL: https://delivr.com/page/753/always-open-by-delivr

Title: Always Open by Delivr.

URL Source: http://delivr.com/page/753/always-open-by-delivr

Markdown Content:
Always Open® is a service that helps businesses sell their products and services to consumers with signage that bridges the divide between storefront windows and online shopping.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Smartphones changed how we moved through stores. Global health crises are changing how we move through the world.

Big box retailers became showrooms for online commerce. Pop-up stores appeared wherever customers gathered. Amazon began “eating the retail world,” accelerating the decline of traditional brick-and-mortar. Digital and physical experiences blurred, and consumers stopped distinguishing between online and offline shopping.

Suddenly, we’re living in a different world.
--------------------------------------------

Widespread pandemics and the threat of infectious pathogens have made digital technology even more integral to daily life. Shoppers increasingly choose mobile ordering, home delivery, curbside, and contact-minimal pickup to protect their health and reduce exposure. These shifts have left a lasting impact on society, pushing brands and retailers to prioritize safer, smarter, and more resilient shopping experiences.

Top 5 ways retail has changed.

➀

Shoppers know as much as salespeople.
-------------------------------------

People used to come into stores with little to no knowledge and relied on a salesperson to advise them on what to buy. Today’s shoppers are doing their online research to find and learn more about the products they are considering and to feel secure about their purchases. **Facilitate their education with smart packaging and smart POS displays with QR codes.**

➁

Relevant suggestions are increasingly coming from a real-time context-aware mobile interaction.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Retailing began with shopkeepers who would welcome in people from the neighborhood and then come to learn about their customers’ needs and preferences. In our constantly connected world, a device is just a proxy for what matters — getting to know your customers. Devices provide context, helping us learn what matters to consumers in a particular location and time. **Layering AR and VR into the online shopping experience enables independent, self-serve shopping that approaches the results of bespoke sales and product sampling.**

➂

Opinions carry more weight than ever.
-------------------------------------

The force of the positive or negative word of mouth has always impacted retail. Social is more important than ever, with online reviews now among the first places a person checks to feel confident about an intended purchase. Real-time feedback and reviews give retailers a defining factor as social media recommendations influence online shoppers. **Do you facilitate reviews and integrate these reviews into your shopping experience?**

➃

Mobile devices drive foot traffic to stores.
--------------------------------------------

Finding the right store — and the product you needed — used to depend on familiarity or serendipity. As the lines have blurred between online and offline, retailers are increasingly integrating mobile into their brick-and-mortar store experience. But what if people can't enter your store or you're closed?;**We suggest having your display window and POS as a gateway to your online store.**

➄

Retail's role in public health safety has changed rapidly during the Coronavirus emergency.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

While mobile devices drive people to stores, disease risk can drive them away. Restaurants are taking the lead in “sanitizing” their establishments. To help slow down the spread of coronavirus on surfaces, restaurants are replacing their paper menus with a QR code on their entrance doors. Some establishments even useQR codes to replace the ordering and payment system. **Further, QR codes reduce the need to interact with sales associates or wait in checkout lines.**

Respond with the perfect mix of impulse, information, and convenience.

QR codes can turn anything, anywhere, into a storefront. This applies to your storefront window, regardless of whether you're open or closed. In a competitive or challenging retail environment, you first need to give your customers a way to act on their interests and learn more immediately. And if they are interested enough to buy it, they should also be able to do that. QR codes can help you with both.

*   **Place a QR code in a newspaper or magazine ad**allowing people to buy the product you advertised directly.
*   **Place one or more QR codes in posters, billboards, or storefront windows, creating a virtual store.** The virtual store concept has been a resounding success for companies like Tesco, a British multinational grocery and general merchandise retailer. It would work like this: 
    1.   Smartphone customers scan QR codes on posters next to pictures of products they want to purchase. The signs in the virtual stores can be designed to resemble a regular store's actual aisles and shelves, making the experience very user-friendly.
    2.   Once the products are scanned, the customer can add them to their shopping cart and pay online once their order is complete.
    3.   Customers schedule a time for home delivery.

*   **Drive people from your storefront window and outdoor signage** to a mobile landing page or online store regardless if you're open or closed.

How does Always Open work?

Always Open is a service that helps businesses promote their products and services to consumers with signage that bridges the divide between posters, billboards, storefront windows, and online shopping. Always Open provides solutions ranging from "Do it yourself" (DIY) to a complete turnkey service.

The Always Open Program includes a mix of services.

*   Consult to discuss your business and evaluate your needs. While independent retailers, franchises, dealerships, and retail chains share common challenges, requirements, and budget impact scope. Twenty minutes is all we'll need.
*   Dynamic QR code(s) redirecting to an existing link of yours, a mobile landing page we host, or an online store we develop with you.
*   Do you need outdoor signage? If you're a DIY type, you can easily design and print them yourself. However, if you need some assistance, we're happy to work with you in any way we can to help you design and print your desired signage.
*   Our printing partners can supply stickers, labels, and even packaging can be facilitated with our printing partners.
*   Dashboard with campaign management & analytics.
*   Ongoing maintenance and management is available self-service or as a managed service.
*   Custom website development with Shopify integration is available.
