BUILD

[Core](https://delivr.com/page/798/core-dynamic-qr-code-generator)[℠](https://delivr.com/page/792/qr-studio-custom-code-design)[Dynamic QR Code Generator](https://delivr.com/page/798/core-dynamic-qr-code-generator)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Delivr’s **Core**℠ Dynamic QR Code Generator empowers enterprises to deploy dynamic, brand-aligned QR codes that can be updated at any time, eliminating costly reprints and maximizing campaign flexibility. It combines enterprise-grade security, 99.9% uptime, GDPR/CCPA compliance, and rich analytics to ensure reliability and global scalability. The solution also supports custom design and export formats to meet diverse print and digital use cases while providing real-time performance insights.

[Explore the Dynamic QR Generator.](https://delivr.com/page/798/core-dynamic-qr-code-generator)

BUILD

[QR Studio℠ Custom Code Design](https://delivr.com/page/792/qr-studio-custom-code-design)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**QR Studio**℠ is a purpose-built creative environment that enables creative teams to design visually striking, fully branded QR codes that go beyond what is offered by generic generators by combining aesthetic control with built-in performance analytics and enterprise-grade compliance. It supports advanced design options — including customizable colors, patterns, logos, Motion QR™, VisualQR™, and ShapeQR™ — while ensuring every code remains fully scannable, GS1-ready, and integrated with real-time analytics for actionable insights. Tailored for high-volume packaging, cross-channel campaigns, and collaborative workflows, QR Studio℠ helps brands turn every scan into a measurable engagement opportunity with governance and workflow controls to support distributed creative teams.

[Explore Custom Code Design.](https://delivr.com/page/792/qr-studio-custom-code-design)

Built for enterprise reliability, these mobile-optimized destinations enable use cases from product information and onboarding to corporate profiles and global localized content. This solution enhances customer experiences by aligning performance, design consistency, and real-world outcomes.

[Explore Mobile Experiences.](https://delivr.com/page/793/nest-mobile-experiences)

Built on a privacy-first foundation, it reinforces brand integrity and streamlines cross-departmental workflows.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Acuity**℠ Intelligent Link Management is a performance-driven link intelligence platform that goes beyond basic URL shortening by enabling automatic routing based on device, location, or language to deliver the most relevant user experience. It supports branded short links and QR codes with customizable domains and visual styles to strengthen brand trust and engagement. Additionally, Acuity provides real-time insights and dynamic link updates on live campaigns without technical complexity.

[Explore Intelligent Link Management.](https://delivr.com/page/797/acuity-intelligent-link-management)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Worldview**℠ Advanced Analytics powers the Measure layer of The Experience Suite layer — turning every interaction into clear, actionable insight, designed privacy-first by design. **Worldview**℠ delivers a comprehensive view of audience engagement by converting scan and click interactions into actionable insights like geographic hotspots, time-based trends, and campaign ROI. The analytics platform supports enterprise decision-making by highlighting patterns, user preferences, and channel performance in real time. With privacy-first, zero-knowledge reporting and integration with tools such as Google Analytics® , it enables brands to optimize strategies without compromising trust.

[Explore Advanced Analytics.](https://delivr.com/page/794/worldview-advanced-analytics)

IDENTIFY & AUTHENTICATE

[Imprint℠ Super Simple Serialization](https://delivr.com/page/789/imprint-super-simple-serialization)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Imprint**℠ Super Simple Serialization transforms traditional serialization by allowing enterprises to quickly generate large volumes of unique, traceable links or QR codes without the need for APIs or infrastructure, scaling from hundreds to millions of identifiers. Each serialized item delivers real-world engagement data and serves as a unique digital identity. This feature enhances product traceability, authentication, and campaign attribution while maintaining privacy-first compliance.

[Explore Super Simple Serialization.](https://delivr.com/page/789/imprint-super-simple-serialization)

IDENTIFY & AUTHENTICATE

[Ring℠ GS1-compliant Digital Link](https://delivr.com/page/791/ring-gs1-compliant-digital-link)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Ring**℠ GS1-compliant Digital Link enables enterprises to replace multiple codes on packaging with a single, ISO-compliant QR code that supports point-of-sale checkout, supply chain traceability, and consumer engagement. By embedding GS1 identifiers into structured URLs, it simplifies operations, reduces scanning errors, and enhances visibility across commerce and logistics. This unified approach also unlocks rich digital experiences and real-time insights while maintaining privacy and compliance.

[Explore GS1-compliant Digital Link.](https://delivr.com/page/791/ring-gs1-compliant-digital-link)

TRUST

This zero-knowledge analytics approach supports brands that prioritize ethical data use while still driving strategic decisions.

[Explore Privacy-First, Zero-Knowledge Architecture.](https://delivr.com/page/790/hush-privacy-first-zero-knowledge)
