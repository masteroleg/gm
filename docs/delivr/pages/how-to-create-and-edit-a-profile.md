# How to create and edit a profile.

URL: https://delivr.com/faq/1608/how-to-create-and-edit-a-profile

Title: How to create and edit a profile.

URL Source: http://delivr.com/faq/1608/how-to-create-and-edit-a-profile

Markdown Content:
Create a sleek, dual-purpose digital business card optimized for mobile use. With Delivr’s Profile feature, you can craft a profile that functions as a personalized bio page. This digital solution emphasizes your brand and links — perfect for networking, sales, or personal branding. It features a clean, tap-friendly design displaying your photo, contact information, and a curated list of links to your website, social profiles, calendar, and more. Instantly shareable via QR code or link, it offers a smart, professional way to connect and stay connected—no app needed.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

How to get started with a profile.

To begin creating a Profile, use either navigation method:

1.   **Click the "Options" link** in the top navigation bar.
2.   Or click the subtle **three-dot icon (…)** just beneath the Start Here URL bar to reveal additional campaign types.
3.   Select **“Create Profile with vCard”** to launch the Page Builder.

Basics

**Profile Type**
----------------

*   Choose **Person** if you're representing yourself as an individual. 
    *   This profile type is designed for individuals and typically includes a profile photo (often a headshot), a brief bio or summary, and contact information. It's commonly used for networking, building a personal brand, or engaging in social and professional interactions. Ideal for platforms such as LinkedIn, Facebook, or Instagram.

*   Choose **Company** if you're setting up a business or organizational presence. 
    *   This profile is meant for **organizations, businesses, or institutions**. It represents a brand or legal entity and is typically managed by one or more individuals on behalf of the company. Ideal for business listings, company pages on platforms like LinkedIn and Facebook, B2B networks, job posting accounts, and for use in marketing, advertising, or sales-related activities.

**Profile Display**
-------------------

*   Web Page with vCard 
    *   Choosing this option provides a hosted landing page featuring your bio, contact details, vCard download, and any additional content you’d like to share.

*   vCard Direct Download Only 
    *   Skip the landing page, this option instantly delivers your vCard for quick and easy contact saving.

**Add Contact Information**
---------------------------

**Add data that will appear on your public profile and in the downloadable vCard:**

*   Name & Business Title
*   Company Name
*   Address (Street, City, ZIP, Country)
*   Work & Mobile Phone
*   Fax
*   Email (choose between a secure form or mailto link)
*   Website URL, Skype
*   Time Zone
*   Contact Icons Color: Anthracite or Custom

Contact details can be enabled or disabled. Enabling **Contact Options** displays action buttons like “Call Mobile”, “Send Email”, and “Download vCard”. Disabled, your contact details will be hidden.

**Media & Branding**
--------------------

*   **Upload Cover Photo** (max 5MB, 1080px wide recommended)
*   **Upload Profile Photo** (square, 1080×1080px)
*   **Media Block URL**: Embed content from YouTube, Vimeo, Instagram, SoundCloud, and others

**Social & Communication Links**
--------------------------------

**Support for major platforms, including:**

*   LinkedIn
*   Facebook, Instagram, 𝕏
*   WhatsApp, Discord, Line
*   YouTube, TikTok, Twitch
*   Calendar Integrations
*   Icon Colors. Brand Colors or Custom Colors

**Links Section**
-----------------

Once your profile is saved, the Links section becomes available for editing. This feature is designed to consolidate multiple links into a single, easy-to-access landing page, ideal for social media bios that typically allow only one link. It’s especially valuable for creators, professionals, brands, and teams who want to organize and share multiple destinations, such as websites, booking pages, Shopify storefronts, product pages, downloads, portfolios, and more—all in one convenient, shareable place. These links appear as tappable buttons beneath your media and contact details, providing your audience with clear, direct pathways to explore your online presence or offerings**.**

**What a Link Button Includes.**
--------------------------------

Each button is made up of the following elements:

*   **Button Label.**The label that appears on the button.
*   **Button Link**. The link the button will take users to when clicked.
*   **Color**. Choose a custom color to match your brand or visual style.
*   **Style**. Select between two presentation styles: 
    *   A filled button with bold presence.
    *   An outlined button for a lighter, minimal look.

**How to Save Link Changes**
----------------------------

**Links are managed individually by row. When editing or adding a link:**

*   Each row must be saved individually by clicking the green check icon.
*   After editing a row, save it before making changes to the next.
*   If you switch to another row without saving, your changes will be lost.

You can use as many links as needed to create a complete, functional link hub.

Modules Tab

**Form**
--------

Attach a form to appear below your profile content.

*   Choose from existing forms or [create a new one].
*   Enables lead capture or direct contact.

**Property**
------------

Display a featured **Property** above your profile content.

*   Ideal for real estate or showcasing locations.
*   Select from existing properties or [create a new property].

Places Tab

**Campaign Location**. Map your campaign to a real-world location to track geographic engagement. **Marker Groups.** Use Marker Groups to organize campaigns by physical locations.

Metadata Tab

**Campaign Details**
--------------------

Use this section to define campaign metadata and reporting options:

*   **Campaign Medium**. Type of advertising (e.g., print, display)
*   **Campaign Source.**Website, channel, or publication
*   **Job #, Ad #, Web ID**
*   **Merchandise Description**

**Campaign Audience & Timing**
------------------------------

*   **Estimated Audience Size**. For calculating response rates.
*   **Campaign Period**. Set start and/or end dates.
*   **Campaign Time Zone.**Use global or custom setting.

**Campaign Creative**
---------------------

*   Upload a **PDF (max 10MB)** of your creative asset for documentation.

How to edit a profile.

To make changes to an existing profile:

1.   Find the **Profile** campaign on your Dashboard.
2.   Click the **Edit (pencil) icon**
3.   Update fields, design, or metadata as needed.
4.   Click **Save & Close** to apply updates.

Use Delivr Profiles to create consistent, branded contact pages across your team or business. Attach forms, link media, and map engagement with powerful campaign tools—all in one profile.
