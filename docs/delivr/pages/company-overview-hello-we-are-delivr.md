DELIVR

Infrastructure for the Connected World.
---------------------------------------

Physical. Digital. Spatial.
---------------------------

### We’re Delivr — a human-centered software and services company helping brands connect people from the world around them to digital and spatial experiences. Our trigger-agnostic, privacy-first platform makes it simple to engage with media, products, objects, and places. Powered by our Engagement Suite — including the QR Code Generator, Super Simple Serialization℠, GS1 Digital Link, and Intelligent Link Routing℠ — Delivr assembles experiences to spark deeper emotional connections between brands and their audiences.
We don’t just follow trends; we set them. Delivr launched in 2008 and was among the first to offer dynamic QR codes. Today, we continue to set the standard for modern, reliable QR infrastructure.

THE DELIVR DIFFERENCE

7 reasons to choose Delivr.
---------------------------

EXCELLENCE

Setting the standard for dynamic QR code generators.

Industry-leading dynamic QR code technology with advanced analytics and ISO compliance.

Over 15-years ago, we established the most comprehensive and reliable [dynamic QR code generator](https://delivr.com/page/798/core-dynamic-qr-code-generator), generating and managing ISO-compliant codes. Today, thousands of businesses rely on Delivr to intelligently route, track, and visualize consumer engagement across print and broadcast media, and packaging. Our industry-leading [analytics](https://delivr.com/page/794/worldview-advanced-analytics) go beyond scan counts, capturing location, device type, trends, and real-time performance to empower you with actionable insights for smarter campaign decisions. [View our Plans & Pricing.](https://delivr.com/plans)

PERFORMANCE

First impressions matter; performance leads the way.

Blazing-fast redirects and landing pages that maximize scans and conversions.

Webpages reached through short URLs or dynamic QR codes often make your company's first impression. It's a digital introduction to your brand and what you offer. Understandably, performance is a foundational aspect of delivering a great user experience. Delivr supercharges your marketing and branding efforts with dynamic QR codes, fast redirects, and [fast-loading landing pages](https://delivr.com/page/793/nest-mobile-experiences) that will delight visitors, persuade prospects, and win customers.

RELIABILITY

Unmatched record of reliability.

QR infrastructure with 99.9% uptime for uninterrupted campaigns.

With a 15-year, 99.9% uptime record, Delivr keeps your dynamic QR codes running when it matters most. Downtime isn't an option. Our platform delivers peace of mind with enterprise-grade performance, scalability, and security. Delivr is built on leading infrastructure from Microsoft Azure®, AWS®, and Cloudflare®. We offer unmatched reliability and robust protection for QR code campaigns of any scale.

SECURITY

Security first, not an afterthought.

Hardened platform protection with continuous testing and best-practice engineering.

Security is critical in today’s threat landscape. That’s why we protect your data and systems across the entire security continuum — through engineering best practices, application security, penetration testing, and regular security assessments. We also maintain strict policies for source code use and infrastructure protection, so you can trust Delivr at every level.

PRIVACY

Privacy is of the utmost importance.

Zero-knowledge data practices with anonymized tracking and full regulatory compliance.

Our privacy-first, zero-knowledge approach sets [a new standard in data protection for the QR Code Generator](https://delivr.com/page/814/delivrs-approach-to-data-privacy) industry. We collect only anonymized location data — never personally identifying information — and never fingerprint devices, build visitor profiles, or track individual activity. IP addresses are anonymized before storage, and our platform is fully GDPR- and CCPA-compliant, keeping your data secure, under your control, and private by design.

SUPPORT

We’ll take amazing care of you. 100%human.

Need help? Real humans are ready when it matters most.

We provide an intuitive suite of products, backed by expert support. From strategy to setup and beyond, our responsive team ensures your dynamic QR code campaigns run flawlessly — so you can focus on results, not troubleshooting. Whether you’re launching a single campaign or managing multiple programs, we’re here to guide you every step of the way.[Contact Support](https://delivr.com/page/652/submit-a-support-request) or call +1.631.750.2785.

EXPERIENCE + SUSTAINABILITY

Established 2008; Made in New York; Built to Last.

Millions of connections each month, powered securely, sustainably, and with purpose.

Since 2008, Delivr has powered hundreds of millions of secure, compliant connections for global brands and enterprises. Our team has built and operated marketing, publishing, and analytics platforms engaging thousands of businesses and millions of people. Delivr holds an A+ carbon rating, outperforming 99% of websites globally, and actively supports initiatives that offset carbon impact as we scale.
