How to add Application URLs to your Campaign.
=============================================

How to add Application URLs to your Campaign.

**Want to send users directly to your mobile app based on their device?**With **Delivr**, you can configure **application-specific URLs** that automatically detect whether a user is on **iOS** or **Android**. This ensures each user gets the right experience—whether it’s a deep link into your app, the App Store, or the Google Play listing.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Steps to add Application URLs.

➀

**Open the Campaign**
---------------------

From your Dashboard, locate the campaign you wish to update.

➁

**Click "Edit Campaign"**
-------------------------

In the campaign row, click the **Edit Campaign** (pencil) icon to access the campaign settings.

➂

**Scroll to the "Application URLs" Section**
--------------------------------------------

Here, you can configure alternate destination URLs based on the user’s device operating system (OS).

➃

**Add Platform-Specific URLs**
------------------------------

You can configure redirections for the following device types:

*   **iPhone / Apple iOS**

 Provide a URL specific to Apple devices—this can be an **App Store link**, an **iOS deep link**, or any other relevant URL.
*   **Android / Android OS**

 Enter a URL tailored to Android users—such as a **Google Play Store link**, an **Android deep link**, or another Android-specific destination.

➄

**Save Your Edits**
-------------------

When you’ve finished updating the Application rules, click **SAVE** or **SAVE & CLOSE** at the top or bottom of the page.

**What Happens for Other Devices?**
-----------------------------------

Devices that don’t match your application-specific rules (e.g., desktop or unsupported OS) will be redirected to your campaign’s **default Destination URL**.

**Clear Application Rules**
---------------------------

You can remove all device-specific rules by clicking **“Clear All”** in the Application URLs section. This will revert all traffic to the default Destination URL.

Pro Tip

Use Application URLs to send users to the right app store or in-app destination without needing multiple QR codes or links, enhancing the user experience and boosting conversions.
