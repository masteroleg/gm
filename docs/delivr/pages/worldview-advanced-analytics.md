*   Track total scans and clicks to measure engagement across all touchpoints.

Audience Heatmap
----------------

Visualize exactly where and how your audience engages.

*   **Geographic Insights**. Plots connections on an intuitive heatmap to see engagement hotspots at the city, region, or country level.
*   **Detailed Engagement Data**. Track timestamps, device types, user actions, and interaction methods to understand how your audience is connecting.
*   **Accuracy You Can Trust**. Verify location precision and ensure insights reflect real-world engagement patterns.
*   **Seamless Analysis**. Export all connections for deeper analysis with your preferred third-party tools, enabling data-driven decisions across campaigns

Location
--------

Understand where your traffic comes from, from countries and states down to cities. This report enables you to identify regional interests, pinpoint areas of engagement concentration, and plan targeted campaigns with confidence.

Trends
------

Spot patterns and plan. Gain a deeper understanding of how your campaigns perform over time. Identify your most active days and weeks to see when your audience is most engaged. Track long-term campaign momentum over months or even up to three years, providing you with the insights to plan strategically for future initiatives. Analyze connections by month and year to uncover seasonal trends and optimize your campaigns for peak performance. With these insights, you can make data-driven decisions that drive sustained growth and engagement.

Time
----

Know exactly when your audience is most active. Delivr Worldview℠ Advanced Analytics shows you not just _how many_ connections, but _when they happen_. See your busiest time of day at a glance, whether afternoon peaks, morning commutes, or evening sessions. With detailed charts by hour, daily patterns, and commuting periods, you’ll know the exact windows when your audience is most responsive.

Calendar
--------

Get a day-by-day view of your campaign performance. The Calendar highlights exactly how many connections happened on each date, making it easy to spot busy days, slow periods, and patterns across weeks or months.

Dayparts
--------

Not all hours are equal. With Delivr Worldview℠ Advanced Analytics, you’ll see exactly which dayparts drive the most engagement — from DOOH & OOH windows (morning commute, daytime, evening) to Television & Connected TV slots (daytime, prime time, late night). In this campaign, connections peaked during the Daytime period.

Engagement Method
-----------------

Engagement Method reveals how your audience chooses to connect—whether through QR codes, short URLs, or NFC taps. See not just the numbers, but the story behind each interaction: the share of total engagement, the response rates, and the moments that matter most. With this insight, you can celebrate real connections, understand what resonates, and craft experiences that inspire action.

Acquisition
-----------

See where your traffic is coming from, including direct links, dark traffic, social media, or chat apps. Understanding the sources driving your campaign helps you measure ROI and refine channel strategies.

Operating System
----------------

Know the tech behind every scan. With insights into Operating Systems and Platforms, you’ll see whether audiences lean towards desktop, mobile, or a mix — helping you optimize campaigns for their preferred experience.

Language
--------

Speak your audience’s language — literally. With Connections by Language, you’ll see which device or browser settings dominate, making it easy to localize messaging and reach people in the language they prefer.

Is your marketing spend working hard enough? With Delivr Worldview℠ Advanced Analytics, you’ll know.

Benchmarking reveals your Response Rate by Marketing Channel and Average Cost per Connection, showing just how much more efficient QR-driven insights can be compared to traditional digital channels. Delivr consistently outperforms major ad platforms, providing a more cost-effective way to track real-world engagement.QR codes don’t just track — they unlock previously hidden insights into audience behavior, from location and time of day to device and language, helping you capture engagement that other channels often miss.

Super Simple Serialization℠, a unique feature of Delivr, enables you to track each product individually, providing complete visibility across the supply chain while ensuring authenticity. Beyond security, it opens the door to personalized experiences for your customers, allowing you to connect with them in meaningful ways and gather actionable insights at the product level. From protecting your brand to optimizing operations, serialization transforms every item into a touchpoint for trust, engagement, and growth.

People First
------------

Every connection is a real person. The People First chart highlights the human side of engagement, helping you see beyond raw numbers. Each scan or view represents an individual with a story, a moment of interest, and a decision to interact with your brand. It’s a reminder that behind every click is a person choosing to connect—not an anonymous statistic. By framing metrics in this way, the People First chart shifts the focus from conversions alone to the authentic human connections that drive meaningful relationships.
