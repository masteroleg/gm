Security by Design
------------------

Security is built into every layer of the Delivr platform. We use modern safeguards, controlled access, and continuous monitoring to protect systems and customer data—so you can deploy with confidence at enterprise scale.

Privacy You Can Rely On
-----------------------

We are committed to responsible, transparent data practices. Delivr is designed to:

*   **Collect only what’s necessary**, applying GDPR principles of data minimization and purpose limitation.
*   **Protect anonymity by default**, processing identifiable data only with explicit consent or legal obligation.
*   **Align with global privacy regulations**, including GDPR and related frameworks.

Compliance & Assurance
----------------------

Delivr supports enterprise compliance and procurement workflows by maintaining clear documentation and repeatable controls. Our Trust Center helps reduce review cycles by giving stakeholders a shared, self‑serve reference.
