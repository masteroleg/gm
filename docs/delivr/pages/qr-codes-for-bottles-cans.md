QR Codes for Bottles & Cans.
============================

QR codes are a powerful addition to beverage packaging. Whether printed on bottles, cans, or labels, they allow brands to share more information and create stronger connections with consumers — both at the point of sale and after purchase.

With limited space on packaging, QR codes turn ordinary labels into smart packaging that informs, educates, and engages.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Add Value with Transparent Information.
---------------------------------------

Today’s consumers want more than just a brand name. They want to know.

*   Nutritional information
*   Calorie count
*   Use of artificial sweeteners
*   GMO status
*   Sustainability practices
*   Carbon footprint details

It’s not always possible to fit all of this on a bottle or can. A QR code can link to a dedicated mobile page with detailed product information. This helps brands stay transparent while keeping packaging clean and simple.

For smaller beverage producers, QR codes can also link to stories from growers, farmers, or production teams — helping them stand out from large corporations and build authenticity.

Using QR Codes on Wine Bottles.
-------------------------------

QR codes on wine bottles can enhance the buying experience. When customers scan in-store or after delivery, they can access:

*   Information about grape origin
*   Introductions from growers or wineries
*   Tasting notes
*   Suggested food pairings
*   Vineyard stories

This added layer of storytelling helps customers feel more confident in their purchase and builds a stronger emotional connection to the brand.

Enhancing Coffee Cups with QR Codes.
------------------------------------

Even takeaway coffee cups can benefit from QR codes. A simple scan can connect customers to.

*   Loyalty programs
*   Discount offers
*   Digital stamp cards
*   Special promotions

While enjoying their drink, customers can easily access rewards or offers, increasing repeat visits and customer retention.

QR codes for bottles and cans transform beverage packaging into an interactive experience. They provide transparency, share brand stories, and support loyalty initiatives — all without redesigning the label. Simple to implement and easy to update, QR codes are becoming an essential tool for modern beverage brands.
