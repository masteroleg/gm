URL Slugs / Custom Back-Halfs
-----------------------------

A URL slug — also called a custom back-half — is the part of a shortened or branded link that appears after the final slash. It helps make links more meaningful, memorable, and easier to recognize.

For example: **qr.yourdomain.com/productname**. In this link, “productname” is the URL slug. Rather than using a random string of characters, a custom slug lets you define this portion of the link so it reflects the content or destination — such as a product name, campaign, location, or promotion.

**Well-designed slugs can:**

*   Make links easier to read and share
*   Improve user trust and click-through rates
*   Help with organization and reporting
*   Support SEO for branded short domains (where applicable)

To get the best results, choose slugs that are short, descriptive, and consistent in style (for example: lowercase, hyphen-separated words).

Campaign Controls
-----------------

Put your links on your terms. Acuity℠ lets you manage what happens before, during, and after a campaign — all without coding or complex workflows.

Expire links on a set date, replace outdated destinations with updated content, display custom messages, or automatically redirect users to fallback pages — ensuring no one ever lands on a broken or irrelevant experience.

With Campaign Controls, every link stays intentional, on-brand, and aligned with your strategy — because your links deserve the same care and craftsmanship as your brand.
