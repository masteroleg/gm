Why is a touchless marketing solution important?
================================================

*   It helps your customers avoid touching POS displays and surfaces.
*   It facilitates contactless in-store engagement.
*   It provides a safe, self-service education and purchase experience.

For consumers and other audiences.
----------------------------------

It is possible for viruses to spread through surfaces. Using a touchless marketing solution provides a safe option for consumers to access the product information they need while avoiding touching surfaces and displays.

For brands & retailers.
-----------------------

Assures people of your safety measures by creating a more thoughtful environment for everyone.
