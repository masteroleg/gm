The Limitations of Basic QR Tools.
----------------------------------

Free QR tools are temporary.
----------------------------

*   **Static QR codes lock you in.** Updates require reprints—even for small changes.
*   **No real visibility.** Basic tools lack analytics, so you can’t see what’s working.
*   **Privacy and data risk.** Free tools often fall short on security and compliance.
*   **Unreliable service.** Low-cost providers can vanish, breaking printed codes overnight.
*   **No item-level control.** Without serialization, you can’t track or manage individual products or locations.
*   **No brand protection.** Basic QR codes are easy to copy, misuse, or divert.

Brands need something built to last.
