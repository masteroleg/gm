How to create a static QR code.
===============================

How to create a static QR code.

Want a straightforward, easy-to-scan QR Code that links directly to a URL or other fixed data? Static QR Codes are ideal for situations where tracking or editing isn’t needed
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**How to navigate to static QR code options.**
----------------------------------------------

**To get started:**

*   Click the **"Options"** link in the top navigation bar,

**OR**
*   Click the subtle **three-dot icon (…)** just below the “Start Here” URL bar to reveal additional campaign types.

**From there, you’ll see two options:**

*   **Static QR Standard Design**
*   **Static QR Custom Design**

**Static QR: Standard Design**
------------------------------

**This is the fastest and most reliable way to create an ISO-compliant QR Code.**An ISO-compliant QR code ensures global interoperability, better readability, and compliance with regulations. By following ISO/IEC 18004 standards, it guarantees consistent performance across platforms, allowing seamless data sharing and dependable scanning on a wide variety of devices and systems. It’s advised for regulated industries, long-term labeling, or any situation where universal scanability is crucial.

**Key Features:**

*   **Data Types Supported.** URL, Text, Email, Phone, SMS, Wi-Fi, Event, Bitcoin
*   **Error Correction Levels.** L (Low), M (Medium), Q (25% – default), H (High)
*   **Margin**. Default is 4 blocks (customizable)
*   **Scale**. Default is 3× (customizable)
*   **Color Options.** Custom code and background colors using HEX values
*   **Contrast Checker.:** Ensures your color choices meet the minimum 4:1 contrast ratio for scan reliability

**Download Formats:**EPS, SVG, PDF, PNG, JPG, TIFF

**Note:** Static QR Codes are **not editable or trackable** once created. The encoded data is permanent.

**Static QR: Custom Design**
----------------------------

**Want a QR Code that visually reflects your brand?**Our Custom Design tool offers extensive creative control—but keep in mind that these QR Codes are not ISO-compliant. Customizations like unique eye shapes, pixel effects, and embedded logos prioritize aesthetics over strict technical standards, making them ideal for marketing and brand-driven use cases.

**Customization Options Include:**

*   **Data Types Supported**. URL, Text, Email, Phone, SMS, Wi-Fi, Event, Bitcoin, WhatsApp, PayPal, Skype, Zoom
*   **Color Palette**. Custom background and code colors
*   **Design Style**. Modify pixel style, eye/corner shapes
*   **Logo Upload**. Add a logo or icon at the center
*   **Frames**.Include a call-to-action frame such as “Scan Me”
*   **Set Options**. Adjust margins, scale, error correction, and contrast

**Static vs. Dynamic QR codes**
-------------------------------

**Feature****Static QR Code****Dynamic QR Code**
**Editable After Creation**❌ No✅ Yes
**Trackable Scans**❌ No✅ Yes
**URL Shortening**❌ No✅ Yes (via Delivr)
**Compliance**✅ Yes (Standard Only)✅ Yes
**Best For**Print, packaging, offline materials Campaigns, analytics, flexible routing

**Why Choose Static QR Codes?**
-------------------------------

*   The data is directly embedded at the time of creation, no hosting or subscriptions required.
*   Doesn’t depend on any cloud platform to work—just scan and go.

Unlike other platforms that only support dynamic QR codes, **Delivr gives you the freedom to create both static and dynamic codes**, so you can choose the best tool for each use case.
