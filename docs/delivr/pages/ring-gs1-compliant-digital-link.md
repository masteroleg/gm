*   Packaging clutter
*   Scanning conflicts at checkout

*   Disconnected data and experiences

**A better way is possible.**

![Image 2: GS1 US Solution Partner](https://imagedelivery.net/XK1P2PjAYi2e8FGhgua6BQ/ddc2c27a-0b2e-48d1-fa8f-7b02e402e700/public)The Solution. Ring℠ GS1-compliant Digital Link by Delivr.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**GS1 Digital Link** embeds a product’s GS1 identifiers (such as GTIN, which is a unique product code; batch/lot, which is used to identify production runs; serial number; or expiration date) directly into a structured web URL — encoded in a single, ISO-compliant QR code.

Ring℠ applies this standard seamlessly.

**One smart, GS1-compliant code that:**

*   Transacts with retail POS systems
*   Enables end-to-end supply chain traceability
*   Unlocks rich, dynamic digital experiences for consumers

**Everything happens with one scan.**

This brings us to a key advantage. One Code. Every Touchpoint.
--------------------------------------------------------------

**Ring℠ replaces multiple codes with a single GS1 Digital Link that works everywhere:**

*   **Checkout Ready**. Fully GS1-compliant and optimized for retail point-of-sale systems.
*   **Supply Chain Visibility**. Track products from production to retail. Detect diversion. Verify authenticity.
*   **Consumer Engagement**. Deliver storytelling, promotions, manuals, sustainability info, loyalty programs, and more — instantly.
*   **Operational Simplicity**. With fewer codes, packaging is cleaner, costs are lower, and scanning errors decrease.

**From production line to purchase, one code connects it all.**
