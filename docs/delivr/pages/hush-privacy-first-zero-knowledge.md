# In a world obsessed with data, we chose privacy.

At Delivr, privacy goes beyond compliance — it’s the foundation of our platform, shaping how we build, operate, and innovate with a zero-knowledge-by-design approach.

Hush℠ ensures every scan is anonymous, secure, and compliant — delivering true zero-knowledge protection, transparent processes, and enterprise-grade reliability for brands that lead with integrity. This is privacy-first analytics in action.

Hush℠ sets a new standard for QR codes and connected experiences — no cookies, no tracking IDs, no shadow analytics.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Drive engagement. Gain insights. Protect privacy at every step.
---------------------------------------------------------------

Most marketing and analytics platforms collect, track, and profile users — creating risk for brands and eroding consumer trust. Delivr takes a different approach, setting a new standard in privacy.

Our privacy-first, zero-knowledge architecture gives brands vital insights from QR codes, links, and connected experiences — without compromising personal data, identities, or consumer trust.

Zero-Knowledge by Design — No Personal Tracking
-----------------------------------------------

Designed to minimize data collection, Delivr assures brands that they deliver experiences that comply with strict privacy standards from day one.

**Every scan, click, and visit remains anonymous:**

*   No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected or stored.
*   No cookies, fingerprinting, or cross-campaign tracking.
*   IP addresses are anonymized prior to storage.
*   No behavioral profiles, retargeting, or look-alike modeling.
*   GDPR-compliant, CCPA-compliant, and global privacy-aligned architecture.

Provide your audience with transparency and value — while respecting their privacy. This commitment to privacy forms the basis for truly actionable insights.

How Hush℠ Works
---------------

Every interaction follows a closed-loop model. Anonymous in, anonymous out, while still giving you the analytics you need to improve.

Actionable Insights — Without Compromise
----------------------------------------

Data that drives decisions — not risk.Brands need analytics that respect privacy.

Delivr proves brands can access vital insights without invasive practices, maintaining performance and upholding ethical standards.Delivr delivers privacy-safe insights, enabling brands to optimize performance with ethical, compliant data — no surveillance or overreach.

**Gain visibility into:**

*   **Timestamp**. Understand time-based engagement trends.
*   **Campaign ID**. Attribute scans and clicks to a specific managed QR or short link for insights into campaign and channel performance.
*   **Approximate Location**. Understand engagement location and delivr localized experiences. Anonymized by default, GPS optional via opt-in.
*   **Type of Device, OS & Browser**. Technical insight into device patterns.
*   **Device Language**. Deliver language-specific experiences.

No individual tracking. No identity stitching. No shadow profiles.

Receive accurate, ethical, decision-ready data for your organization. This strong security foundation is central to protecting user trust.

Security That Strengthens Privacy
---------------------------------

Because Delivr does not store sensitive user identifiers, you benefit from less data to secure and nothing exploitable.

**Our infrastructure includes:**

*   **Encryption Everywhere**. Encryption in transit and at rest. AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.2/1.3 in transit.
*   **Data Minimization**. Only essential metrics are collected, nothing more.
*   **Self-Destructing Analytics**. Strict retention periods automatically erase old data.
*   **Zero AI Usage**. We never use AI or machine learning on your analytics data.
*   **Enterprise-Grade Security**. Continuous monitoring with enterprise-grade availability and reliability.
*   **Control without compromise**. Your reports are private by default, you share only what you choose when you choose, and you can delete your data anytime — instantly and permanently.

Combining security and zero-knowledge principles, Delivr mitigates risk, helping your brand meet marketing and compliance obligations with confidence. This positions Delivr as an ideal choice for brands that value trust.
