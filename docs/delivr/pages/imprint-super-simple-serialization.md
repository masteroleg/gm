Imprint. Serialization used to be complex. Now it’s simple.
===========================================================

Serialization is essential. Modern brands need unique digital identities for every product, print piece, and touchpoint — without the cost or complexity of traditional systems.

Imprint℠ Super Simple Serialization transforms ordinary links and QR codes into powerful, privacy‑first digital identifiers. Every item gets its own story. No integrations. No APIs. No infrastructure. Just an instant, scalable identity that works seamlessly across the Delivr platform.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Effortless identity at scale.
-----------------------------

Unlike other solutions, you can create hundreds, thousands, or millions of unique, traceable links or QR codes in minutes with Imprint℠. Instantly access real‑world insights into item engagement, unmatched by legacy systems.

**No infrastructure or code — just results.**

From product to person.
-----------------------

Each code proves engagement, authenticity, and impact. With Imprint℠, every interaction delivers measurable value.

Use Super Simple Serialization to:
----------------------------------

*   Simplify product registration and authentication.
*   Enable item‑level tracking across packaging, inventory, and retail.
*   Power hyper‑personalized and localized experiences
*   Attribute influencers, channels, and campaigns with precision
*   Transform static print into interactive, measurable media.
*   Strengthen brand protection with unique, serialized codes that help deter counterfeiting and enable authenticity checks.

Serialization, simplified.
--------------------------

Forget complex systems, APIs, and integrations—or managing countless QR codes.

With Imprint℠, all you need is a spreadsheet.

Add unique IDs to your Experience URL, send to print or NFC partners, and start variable printing or encoding in minutes.

**That’s it. Super Simple Serialization.**

**Turn a single Experience URL like https://delivr.com/ABC123 into unlimited serialized links or QR codes:**

*   https://delivr.com/ABC123/001
*   https://delivr.com/ABC123/002
*   https://delivr.com/ABC123/003

Each is tracked independently and in aggregate within Worldview℠ Advanced Analytics.

Privacy‑first by design.
------------------------

Imprint℠ ensures serialization occurs solely within Delivr’s privacy‑first platform, so customer data never leaves your environment — providing immediate compliance with GDPR and enterprise policies and setting it apart from other systems.

No limits. No bottlenecks.
--------------------------

Self‑provision serialization instantly, no matter your scale. Unlike API-based systems, Imprint℠ reduces setup time by more than 90%, with no APIs to configure or dependencies to manage — so it moves at your business's speed.

Every serialized connection is compliant, measurable, and secure by default.

*   **Instant Traceability**. Follow every item’s journey in real time
*   **Brand Protection**. Detect diversion, counterfeits, and unauthorized resellers
*   **Smart Personalization**. Deliver dynamic content per individual scan
*   **Total Simplicity**. No extra tools or technical setup required

Serialization that connects everything.

Imprint℠ stands apart by bridging identity, engagement, and intelligence — giving brands unique clarity, control, and connections at scale that traditional serialization cannot match.

We can think of many remarkable uses for serialization; here are three.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

➀

Physical products.
------------------

Make product registration easier. Super Simple Serialization eliminates the need to enter or search for a serial number manually. By scanning a QR code, a serial number can be filled in automatically on a registration page.

➁

Digital communications.
-----------------------

Increase the effectiveness of your sales efforts by making your digital (or print) communications even more personal. Super Simple Serialization bridges the divide between mass marketing and personalized engagement.

➂

Smart objects.
--------------

Instead of generating and managing thousands or millions of managed Short links or QR codes, use Super Simple Serialization to power a serialized link that passes the object's identity to your service or webpage. Serialize product packaging, direct mail, posters, POS materials, equipment, and parts with a unique identifier.

Other uses for serialization.
-----------------------------

*   Improve inventory visibility by serializing your inventory, giving each piece of merchandise a unique digital identity - its digital twin.
*   Assign individual serial numbers to printed items to facilitate personalized URLs.
*   With our DIY product serialization feature, you can easily track the sales of your products across different regions and identify any areas where they may be appearing despite not being distributed there. Our reporting system provides you with valuable insights into the performance of your products in various markets.
*   Serialize the links to your campaigns so the social influencer can be attributed.
*   Print a serialized QR code on a car rental receipt, making it easier for customers to reach their personalized rental landing page.
*   Manage single-use coupons and promotions.
