Meet the QR Code Reader
-----------------------

Scan QR codes directly in your browser—no apps, no setup, no tracking, no friction. Just point, scan, and go.

Integration is as simple as adding a link to your mobile site. One tap launches a web-based QR reader that scans instantly—directly from the browser, on any smartphone. No app downloads required. Just point the camera, and QR codes are recognized in real time from the live video feed.

Top retail use cases.
---------------------

If your customers don’t have your app—or if your experience runs in the browser rather than a native app—you can still drive engagement by enabling QR scanning directly on your website. This approach supports a range of use cases, including:

*   Product lookup
*   Product purchase
*   Product registration
*   Launch AR experiences and product demos
*   Scan loyalty cards
*   Gift registry

Customization for brands and businesses.
----------------------------------------

*   Available fully hosted and branded with your logo.
*   Embeddable on your mobile website or campaign landing page. Scanning can be limited to only your authorized QR codes.
*   This tool offers features to help protect your brand, verify product authenticity, and detect instances of counterfeit and unauthorized sales.
