Understanding the Brand Kit.
============================

Understanding the Brand Kit.

The Brand Kit organizes and manages your brand’s visual identity across all campaigns, QR codes, landing pages, and profiles. You can upload logos, choose brand colors, and apply consistent styling elements to keep a unified look across all assets. To access it, click the Brand link at the top of the page (to the right of Archive). You will see five tabs; each covers a different aspect of your brand's appearance within Delivr.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Design

This tab controls the **design defaults** for your campaign experiences. Upload your logo, define brand colors, and manage key design elements. Here you can define:

*   Interstitial Page Design
*   Apple Touch Icon
*   Device GPS Prompt Screen
*   QR Code Casing: Uppercase or Lowercase
*   QR Code Default Color
*   Default Page Link Color

Don’t forget to click **Save All Design Settings** when you're done.

2. Pages

Apply consistent branding across **all your Pages**.

*   **Logo** (universal logo for top of all pages) 
    *   Upload logo (Max 500kB, 1080px wide recommended)

*   **Footer Customization**
    *   Footer text
    *   Background & Text Color

*   **Social Media Icons**

Include links to: 
    *   LinkedIn, Facebook, X (Twitter), Instagram
    *   WhatsApp, Line, Discord
    *   YouTube, TikTok, Twitch
    *   Calendar and more

*   **Icon Color Settings**

Use **brand colors** or define **custom colors** for social icons.

Click **SAVE**to confirm changes.

3. Profiles

Apply consistent branding across **all your Profile pages**.

*   **Organization Name & Logo**
    *   Logo Upload (Max 500kB, 1080px recommended)
    *   Optionally, add a URL link to the logo

*   **Main Copy & Footer**
    *   Custom messaging
    *   Footer customization with color options

*   **Social Media & Icon Colors**

 Same categories and customization as in the Pages tab.

Click **SAVE**to confirm changes.

4. QR Logos

Upload and manage **logos used within QR code templates**.

*   These logos are available for use within the QR Code Designer.

5. QR Templates

Access and manage **template designs for QR codes**:

*   Create consistent QR code visuals for different campaigns
*   Templates can include pre-set colors, frames, logos, and more.

Templates saved here can be reused across various campaigns for speed and brand consistency.
