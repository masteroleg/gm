Delivr’s Commitment to a Greener and More Sustainable Future
============================================================

At Delivr, we’re not just a secure and privacy-first platform for generating QR codes. We contribute to the sustainability movement by building a platform that helps make the world greener with every QR code, scan, and page at a time. Today, we are pleased to share our achievements. Delivr’s web pages, landing pages, and profiles achieve a carbon rating of A+, surpassing 98% of all pages worldwide. This contribution sets us apart in our industry and underscores our mission to promote sustainability, innovation, and responsibility.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We have created an eco-friendly and efficient platform through sustainable energy, thoughtful optimization, performance techniques, and privacy-first practices of zero use of social media trackers, cross-site tracking cookies, and content trackers.

When you visit any Delivr-hosted landing or profile page, you become part of something wonderful. Our pages produce a mere 0.02–0.03 grams of CO2 per visit. Over a year at our current usage, that’s the CO2 equivalent of boiling water for 267,264 cups of tea. Delivr has committed to investing in tree planting projects to offset this CO2. In comparison, a typical page delivered within the industry generates significantly more carbon emissions than you might expect, 0.8 grams CO2 equivalent per page or close to 27x greater than a Delivr-hosted page. We hope this positive impact on the environment inspires and motivates brands to join us in our mission.

Why This Matters for Brands.
----------------------------

When a brand chooses Delivr, it’s not just about adopting QR codes — it’s about embracing a sustainable approach to customer engagement. QR codes are already a great tool for minimizing physical waste, but by partnering with Delivr, brands take that commitment a step further. Our platform ensures that every interaction with your QR codes contributes to a greener planet. We achieve this by optimizing our platform for energy efficiency and investing in tree planting projects to offset the CO2 emissions. These efforts enhance our client’s image and appeal to eco-conscious consumers.

For instance, think about the materials you use for marketing, such as posters, packaging, or business cards. When you integrate Delivr QR codes into these materials, the narrative shifts from mere convenience to a broader story about sustainability. For example, a poster with a Delivr QR code can lead to a digital brochure, reducing the need for printed materials. Every scan and visit to a Delivr-powered page demonstrates your commitment to reducing carbon footprints. It’s not about what QR codes can do — it’s about how Delivr makes every interaction more meaningful and environmentally friendly.

How We Do It.
-------------

Our success in reducing CO2 emissions comes from relentless innovation:

*   **Sustainable Energy**. All our web pages run on servers powered by carbon-neutral, renewable energy sources, minimizing our reliance on fossil fuels.
*   **Optimized Performance**. We’ve engineered our platform to be lightweight and efficient, ensuring that every redirect and every page load is fast and eco-friendly.
*   **Data-Driven Insights**S. We constantly analyze and refine our systems to meet the highest sustainability standards.

A Shared Responsibility.
------------------------

Creating a sustainable future is a shared responsibility. By choosing Delivr, you enhance your digital presence and contribute to the global movement to reduce environmental impact. It’s a simple choice with significant implications, and we’re here to guide you every step of the way. Together, we can lead the way in reshaping how businesses and individuals interact with technology.

Let’s Build a Greener Future.
-----------------------------

The journey toward sustainability doesn’t have to be complicated. With Delivr, you’re making a statement that innovation and environmental responsibility go hand in hand. It’s a straightforward yet impactful step that we continuously work to support.

Let us continue to set the standard for sustainable digital experiences. Together, we can ensure that every scan, click, and interaction leaves a lighter footprint on our planet.
